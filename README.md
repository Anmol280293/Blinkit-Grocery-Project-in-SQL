
# Blinkit Sales & Customer Insights Analysis

>  Transforming raw grocery data into actionable business insights using **SQL + Power BI**

---

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Blinkit's grocery sales data** to uncover insights related to:

* Sales performance
* Customer satisfaction
* Inventory distribution

The goal is to help businesses **optimize operations, improve customer experience, and drive revenue growth** using data-driven decisions.

---

##  Business Problem

Blinkit operates in a highly competitive quick-commerce market.

However, key questions remained unanswered:

* Which products generate the most revenue?
* How does outlet type and size impact sales?
* What factors influence customer satisfaction?

 Without clear insights, **decision-making becomes reactive instead of strategic**.

---

##  Project Goal

To build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that:

* Tracks key KPIs
* Identifies high-performing products & outlets
* Analyzes customer behavior
* Supports business optimization strategies

---

##  Key KPIs

*  **Total Sales** → Overall revenue generated
*  **Average Sales** → Revenue per transaction
*  **Number of Items** → Total items sold
*  **Average Rating** → Customer satisfaction score

---

##  Analysis Breakdown

### 1️ Sales by Fat Content

* Compared Low Fat vs Regular products
* Identified impact on revenue & customer ratings

 **Insight:** Certain fat categories drive higher sales volume.

---

### 2️ Sales by Item Type

* Analyzed top-performing product categories

 **Insight:** A few categories contribute to majority of revenue (Pareto effect).

---

### 3️ Outlet Performance by Fat Content

* Compared product performance across outlets

 **Insight:** Product preference varies by outlet type.

---

### 4️ Sales by Outlet Establishment

* Analyzed performance based on outlet age/type

 **Insight:** Older outlets tend to generate more consistent revenue.

---

### 5️ Sales by Outlet Size

* Small vs Medium vs Large outlets

 **Insight:** Medium-sized outlets often deliver optimal performance.

---

### 6️ Sales by Location

* Tier-wise geographic analysis

 **Insight:** Tier-based demand variation highlights expansion opportunities.

---

### 7️ All Metrics by Outlet Type

* Combined KPI comparison across outlet categories

 **Insight:** Supermarket-type outlets dominate in both revenue & item variety.

---
